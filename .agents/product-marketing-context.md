# Meeton ai — Product Marketing Context

## Product
**Meeton ai** is a Japanese B2B AI SDR (Sales Development Representative) platform that converts website leads into booked sales meetings. Built by **DynaMeet, Inc.** (Tokyo, founded 2024-10-03). EN legal name; JA legal name is DynaMeet株式会社.

### 4 Modules
1. **Meeton Live** — AI chat that converses with website visitors in real time and books meetings (replaces scenario-based chatbots)
2. **Meeton Email** — AI replies to inbound emails and books meetings without human SDR intervention
3. **Meeton Calendar** — Smart scheduling that integrates with Google Calendar / MS Teams / Zoom, no back-and-forth
4. **Meeton Library** — AI-powered content library that nurtures returning leads via personalized recommendations

### Positioning
- **"営業のラストワンマイル"** (the last mile of sales) — bridges the gap between marketing-generated leads and human SDRs
- Speed to Lead: **42 hours → 5 seconds** initial response time
- Replaces scenario-based chatbots (ChatPlus, KARTE, sinclo, etc.) with AI that understands intent and routes to meetings
